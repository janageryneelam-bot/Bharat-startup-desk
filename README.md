# 🇮🇳 Bharat Startup Desk

## Your AI Co-Founder for Building Businesses in India

Bharat Startup Desk is an AI-powered assistant designed to simplify the journey of starting and growing a business in India.

Instead of navigating multiple government websites, regulations, and consultants, entrepreneurs receive personalized guidance on registrations, compliances, funding opportunities, licenses, and business planning—all in one platform.

---

## 🚀 Live Demo

https://startup-desk-india.emergent.host

---

## 🎯 Problem

Every year thousands of founders struggle with:

- Choosing the correct business structure
- Understanding GST requirements
- Registering under Startup India & MSME
- Finding government schemes
- Knowing required licenses
- Building a compliance roadmap

Most information is scattered across different government portals.

---

## 💡 Solution

Bharat Startup Desk combines AI with startup knowledge to provide founders with personalized guidance based on:

- Business idea
- Industry
- State
- Investment
- Business stage

---

# ✨ Features

## 🔍 Explore My Idea

Generates:

- Business structure recommendation
- Required registrations
- Licenses
- GST applicability
- Government schemes
- Estimated investment
- Risks
- 12-month roadmap

---

## 📊 Validate My Idea

Analyzes:

- Market demand
- Competition
- Scalability
- Revenue model
- Growth potential
- Investment readiness

---

## 🏢 Already Registered Business

Provides guidance for existing businesses including:

- Compliance checklist
- Funding opportunities
- Business improvement recommendations
- Next growth steps
